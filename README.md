# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for using Boxing Timer.

This Privacy Policy explains how Boxing Timer handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Boxing Timer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app does not require user registration, accounts, or login credentials.

## Data Usage

All timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to us or to any third parties.

## Analytics and Advertising

Boxing Timer does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, advertising networks, or third-party marketing tools.

We do not track your activity inside or outside the app.

## Third-Party Services

Boxing Timer does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Boxing Timer does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

## Data Security

Because Boxing Timer does not collect or transmit user data, no personal information is stored on our servers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the application's support website.
